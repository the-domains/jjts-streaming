  • Geographic Range: Streaming over the web
    gives you
    unlimited

    anywhere
    in the world
    with an internet connection!
  • Convenience: You could for example deliver
    an internal
    training

    the
    associated overheads. You
    could also make
    the
    sessions
    available ‘on demand’

    anyone

  • Wider Audiences: With a web stream you
    can potential
    reach
    anyone with an internet
    connection. If you’re aiming
    to
    break into
    new markets geographically or
    demographically it’s an incredibly
    slick, easy

    someone about

The possibilities are endless – from training
